How to Fundraise for UVFA
Want to make an even bigger impact? Organize a fundraiser for UVFA! Whether it’s a birthday, school event, or online campaign, you can raise funds and awareness for animals.

How to get started:
  1. Choose your fundraising activity or idea.
  2. Email us at uvfa.org@gmail.com to share your plan, and get guidance and support on setting up and managing the fundraiser.
  3. Spread the word—invite friends, family, and your community.
  4. Send us photos, updates, and results so we can celebrate you!

We’ll help you every step of the way and even feature your story on our blog and socials!
